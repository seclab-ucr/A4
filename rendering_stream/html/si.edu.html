Welcome

The Smithsonian Institution is the world's largest museum, education, and research complex. We are a community of learning and the opener of doors. Join us on a voyage of discovery andexplore and learnonline.

Visit

As a public health precaution due to COVID-19, all Smithsonian museums and the National Zoo are closed to the public as of Monday, Nov. 23. Outdoor gardens remain open, no passes required.

Even when we are closed, you can visit us from home. Learn how Smithsonian cares and explore our digital resources, including online events, exhibitions, and podcasts. You can also find curated museum-inspired gifts at our online store.
