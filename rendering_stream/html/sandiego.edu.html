Taylor Wong 19

Taylor Wong 19 (BS/BA) took advantage of nearly every opportunity during her years at USD, because during her senior year of high school she wasnt sure shed even be attending college due to a brain injury. But she didnt give up. I fought harder to get back to where I was, catch up in my classes and graduate in order to attend college. That experience taught me to be resilient and to never give up hope.

Read Taylor's Story
