About TWiT Podcasts

TWiT is named after This Week in Tech, our marquee podcast featuring roundtable discussions with top tech journalists. TWiT.tv podcasts cover tech from every imaginable angle. Theres a show for Android lovers, Apple and iPhone fans, and the enterprise IT techie. Listen to our podcasts on Windows deep-dives, cybersecurity, IoT, open-source, and Google. Youll find reviews on smartphones and laptops, recommendations on the best apps, plus help and how-to with your tech questions. We even have shows on fitness and tech; and photography basics.

Listen, learn, and laugh as our expert podcasters lead discussions and share perspectives. Leo Laporte is well known as The Tech Guy and leads This Week in Tech. Jason Howell hosts All About Android. Mikah Sargent shares his iOS and IoT expertise on iOS Today and Smart Tech Today. Ant Pruitt brings his unique voice to This Week in Google, and more. Our podcasts are in both audio and video formats and are free and ad-supported.
