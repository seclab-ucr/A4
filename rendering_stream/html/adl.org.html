ADL Calls for Improved Hate Crime Reporting

Findings from the just released FBIs annual Hate Crime Statistics Act report reveal total hate crime incidents rose to 7,314 in 2019, a 2.7% increase from last year. Hatred toward one individual hurts an entire community. Reporting hate crimes is critical to create a full understanding of a situation, prevent its occurrence and heal the wounds it opens.
