    GV Black x Coachella Designer Collabs

    Our second drop of Black Lives Matter inspired threads for GV Black, created by four Black designers based in California.
    by Bricks & Wood
    It Takes A Village is a mantra that lives in the heart of Leimert Park courtesy of the Harun Coffee family. Being alumni of the ITAV foundation, it was only right to bring this fantastical celebration of Black culture & music to life through merchandise. 100% Unisex Cotton Jersey Long Sleeve Tee.
    by Diana Boardley
    "I am honored to bring a consciousness with love to Coachella merchandise and the power of the BLM Movement. Giving back to communities and creating a platform for designers to share their truth is my heart and soul - the respect and honor for Black Lives is the promise of my life."
    by Nicholas Mayfield
    "As a Riverside County native, I am proud to share my art on my hometown festival's platform. My vision is to make differences common and to make visuals bigger than words."
    by Supervsn
    In 2018, a group of friends of mine carried a big six foot BLACK LIVES MATTER flag around Coachella for 3 days straight and somehow they didnt make it to the main stage to open up for Beyonce so this is me doing my part to continue the movement for ALL BLACK LIVES, even if they cant rap or sing as good as Beyonce and Im happy to collab with Coachella to amplify our 400 year fight for JUSTICE & EQUALITY.
