ModCloth

ModCloth exists for women who defy convention. Because you’re not just a customer to us. You’re a member of a close-knit, loving, supportive community. And that community isn’t afraid to express themselves, by using their personal style to stand out in a sea of sameness. We offer true vintage-inspired women’s clothing, in playful prints and unique silhouettes. Our clothes are for everybody and every body — with products available in a diverse size range, from 00–28, including plus sizes.Our unique assortment is available exclusively online, where you can chat with ModStylists® for complimentary fit and styling consultations.

ModCloth is a one-stop shop for one-of-a-kind styles ranging from casual dresses to wedding dresses, cozy sweaters to pulled-together blouses, shoes and handbags, to home decor. We pay attention to every detail, because we believe that thoughtfully designed clothing should stand the test of time. We pay homage to iconic styles and silhouettes of every era, especially with our in-house swimwear. We believe that our clothes can make your everyday special and expressive, with dresses that flatter you, versatile tops and pants, and flirty skirts.

ModCloth makes shopping for workwear, weekend outfits, party dresses, and formal clothes so easy and more importantly, fun! While we view this site as a place for pretty outfits, we also like to think of it as a space for our customers to express themselves. We believe that our dresses, separates, accessories, and shoes mean so much more than just style; they’re opportunities to express your best self and live your best life. And we have an entire community cheering on our customers’ individuality and self-expression through our tops, bottoms, and amazing accessories.

So, are you ready to stun in our special occasion dresses, take a spin on the dance floor in our bridesmaids dresses, bundle up in our coats and jackets, and walk the world over in our shoes? Stand out in a sea of sameness with ModCloth.
