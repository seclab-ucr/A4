Your path to 2021: A guide for year 12 students

Even at the best of times, your final year at school can be stressful. The last few months have left a lot of students feeling uncertain about their future studies.

We bring our island campus to you

Be inspired, informed and supported by the best of Tasmania. Access our island campus through a growing number of online courses.

Create your own study experience

With a double degree, you can choose two exciting courses and graduate with a unique edge.
