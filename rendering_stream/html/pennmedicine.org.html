Telemedicine Services at Penn Medicine

We offer telemedicine visits for a variety of services, including coronavirus screenings, select new-patient appointments and follow-up care. More than 9,000 members of our clinical staff are trained to provide telemedicine visits and participate in thousands of virtual visits per day across the health system.
