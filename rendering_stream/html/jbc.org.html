ASBMB journals will be fully open access in 2021

The ASBMB is pleased to announce that the society’s three journals — Journal of Biological Chemistry, Molecular & Cellular Proteomics and Journal of Lipid Research — will be fully open access beginning in January 2021.

Beginning 11/1/2020, authors who submit to the journal, and whose papers are accepted for publication, will pay an article publishing charge: US$2,000 for ASBMB members and US$2,500 for nonmembers.These papers will be published under a CC-BY license.
