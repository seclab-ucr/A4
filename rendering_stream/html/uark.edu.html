Coronavirus Updates

The University of Arkansas continues to work closely with federal and state public health officials to support the health and safety of our campus community. The university’s COVID-19 Response website has the latest updates on safety measures and guidelines for our campus community, including testing and quarantine information, disinfecting protocols and health, academic and event resources available for all students, faculty and staff.

Research Identifies Nanoscale Effect of Water and Mineral Content on Bone

Research into the effect of water and mineral content on collagen fibrils will aid in the development of synthetic materials to mimic bone.

Trio of Food Science Doctoral Students First and Second in Cereals and Grains Contests

Rebecca Bruce, Annagret Jannasch and Ana Gonzalez, all doctoral students in food science, finished either first or second in national contests held by the Cereal & Grains Association.

Pryor Center's Series Concludes With Presentation on Body-Worn Cameras, Police Accountability

Natalie Todak, assistant professor in the Department of Criminal Justice at the University of Alabama at Birmingham, for a presentation on "Body-Worn Cameras and Police Accountability: High Hopes, Lackluster Evidence."
