Summer research program for high-schoolers 

                                                
Applications to our Summer Science Research Program are now open for all high school students, aged 16 and older. From June 28 through August 5, students will spend about 20 hours per week engaging in home-based, mentored activities designed to promote a deepened understanding of biomedical research and its relationship with society. Submit your application by January 4, 2021 at 11:59 p.m.
