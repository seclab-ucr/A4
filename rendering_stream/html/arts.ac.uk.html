And we are top 2 in the world for Art and Design (QS World University Rankings 2020). 



Come and join the creators and innovators of tomorrow.

                    UAL graduates take top 2 in Taylor Wessing Photographic Portrait Prize 2020
                
    
                
            
            
    
                Two UAL talents have won first and second place in the Taylor Wessing Photographic Portrait Prize 2020: Alys Tomlinson who studied Professional Photographic Practice at Central Saint Martins and Lydia Goldblatt, who studied MA Photography at London College of Communication.

                    Hacking Greta Thunberg at the Shared Campus Summer School
                
    
                
            
            
    
                An online Summer School as part of the Shared Campus project. Exploring the theme Pop Cultures, students and staff from 5 partner institutions came together in June/July to Hack the global pop icon, Greta Thunberg.

                    James Purnell joins UAL as President & Vice-Chancellor
                
    
                
            
            
    
                James Purnell has been appointed President & Vice-Chancellor of University of the Arts London, succeeding Sir Nigel Carrington in the role. He joins UAL as President Elect on 1 March 2021 after seven years at the BBC, most recently as Director, Radio & Education.
