      Information, advice and phone and online support services are available.

      • Australia on track for first COVID-19 vaccinations in March 2021

        The COVID-19 vaccine regulatory approvals process is underway and a decision is expected in January.

      • One-off COVID-19 payment to assist travel agents

        Payment will help ensure the sectors sustainability for Australian travellers.

      • HomeBuilder program extended to 31 March 2021, with adjusted conditions

        Program is expected to drive demand for building work and is a key part of Australias Economic Recovery Plan.

      • National Cabinet endorses the Australian COVID-19 Vaccination Policy

        The policy sets out the roles and responsibilities of the Commonwealth, states and territories in rolling out COVID-19 vaccinations.

      Mental health resources

      Its especially important to take care of your mental health and wellbeing during coronavirus.

      Find useful information and resources on theHead to Health website.

      For personal crisis and mental health support you can contact Lifeline on 13 11 44 or Beyond Blue on 1800 512 358 at any time.

      You can also checkemergency support and assistance.

      Have a look at the Hows your head today? campaign resources.

      Getting tested for coronavirus (COVID-19)

      If you need to get tested for COVID-19, you have a few different options. Depending on where you live, you may be able to visit a:
      - Pop up testing centre
      - GPs office
      - Respiratory clinic
      - Drive through testing site
